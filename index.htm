About Me

Hi There!

My name is Emiliano Onorati and I'm a highly motivated Front End Developer with a great passion for
UX and Web Design.

I'm always looking for new things to learn, with Google as my teacher and Notepad++ as my notebook. Web is my life, exploring web technologies and techniques, developing cool web apps. Anything that can help me in expand my knowledge is always welcome.

My Motto in work (as in my life) is to "Take the impossible, make it obvious" - give me a worthy cause and I'll make it happen.

Hope you will enjoy it :)
